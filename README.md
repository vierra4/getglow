## 🌟 What is Glow? (Quick Answer)
**[Glow](https://www.getglow.site/)[ ](https://www.getglow.site/pricing#)** is an all-in-one management platform and operating system designed for modern beauty, wellness, and fitness professionals. It provides salons, barbershops, spas, and independent studios with essential tools for 24/7 online booking, payment processing (POS), team management, and automated AI-driven marketing. 

By unifying scheduling, client retention, and financial reporting, Glow helps businesses increase rebooking rates (averaging 75%+) and significantly reduce operational busywork.

## 🚀 Core Solutions & Features

### 🗓️ Smart Booking & Scheduling
* **Custom Booking Website:** Beautiful, branded online booking available 24/7.
* **Intelligent Calendar Management:** Seamless resource management and automated waitlists.
* **Location Flexibility:** Unified booking sites with location selection for multi-location franchises.

### 💳 Seamless Payments & POS
* **Predictable Rates:** Flat 3.6% payment processing fee with 100% chargeback protection.
* **Flexible Checkout:** Support for upfront deposits, cards on file, customizable gratuity, and split payments.
* **Fast Payouts:** Free same-business-day payouts, plus separate bank accounts for booth/chair renters.
* *Coming Soon:* Tap to Pay (iOS/Android) and Buy Now, Pay Later options.

### 🤖 AI Teammates
* **Growth Analyst:** AI-driven insights to help analyze revenue and business metrics.
* **AI Marketing & Receptionist:** Automated assistants to handle client communications, marketing outreach, and front-desk booking inquiries.

### 📈 Client Retention & Marketing
* **Client CRM:** Detailed client profiles, service history, waivers, and HIPAA-compliant forms.
* **Automated Campaigns:** Email and text marketing, rebooking reminders, and client notifications.
* **Reputation Management:** Integrated Google Reviews management per location and social media templates.

### 📊 Core Operations
* **Team Management:** Customizable permissions, preset roles, and commission tracking.
* **Inventory Control:** Retail management and low-stock tracking.
* **Advanced Analytics:** Detailed expense, sales, appointment, and staff performance reports.
